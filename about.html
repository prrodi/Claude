The Studio

Our
Story.

Founded 2016

Built on
Obsession.

Apex Auto Detailing was founded on a simple belief: that a vehicle isn't just transportation — it's an investment, a passion, and a reflection of its owner's standards.

What started as a solo operation in a rented garage has grown into Los Angeles' most trusted luxury detailing studio. Eight years, 500+ vehicles, and a reputation built on nothing but results.

We don't take on every vehicle. We take on the ones we can truly do justice to — and we treat every single one as if it were our own.

Studio Interior
Our Mission

We Treat Vehicles
Like Works of Art.

Precision is not a selling point for us — it's a minimum standard. Every detail matters: the angle of the polishing pad, the temperature of the ceramic cure, the final inspection under studio lighting. We obsess over the things most people never notice — because that's what separates good from exceptional.

Our mission is simple: to provide the finest automotive detailing experience available, delivered with transparency, expertise, and an uncompromising commitment to perfection.

Our Values
