FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your appointment.

How long does a full detail take?
+
It depends on the package and vehicle condition. A Basic detail takes 1–2 hours, Premium takes 3–4 hours, and an Elite detail with paint correction can take a full day (6–8 hours). We'll provide an accurate time estimate at booking.
Do you offer mobile detailing?
+
Yes — we offer mobile detailing for Basic and Premium packages within a 25-mile radius of our studio. Mobile service requires access to water and electricity at your location. A small travel fee may apply based on distance.
What's the difference between waxing and ceramic coating?
+
Wax is a temporary surface protectant lasting 1–3 months that adds shine and light hydrophobic properties. Ceramic coating is a semi-permanent nano-technology solution that chemically bonds to paint, providing superior UV, chemical, and water protection for 1–5+ years.
How should I prepare my car before the appointment?
+
Simply remove personal belongings from the interior and let us handle the rest. There's no need to pre-wash — in fact, we prefer you don't, as an improper wash before our decontamination process can introduce additional swirl marks.
Is there a satisfaction guarantee?
+
Absolutely. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our work, contact us within 48 hours of your appointment and we'll re-address it at no charge. Your car's perfection is our reputation.
