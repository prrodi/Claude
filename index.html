How We Work

The Apex Process

Every vehicle goes through a rigorous 5-stage protocol — no shortcuts, no compromises.

🔍
Inspection

Full paint depth analysis, panel assessment, and defect mapping under high-intensity lighting.

🧼
Preparation

Safe pre-wash, foam decontamination, iron removal, and clay bar treatment.

🔬
Correction

Multi-stage machine polishing under dual-LED lighting to eliminate all defects.

💎
Protection

Ceramic coating, wax, or PPF application — sealed in a dust-free environment.

🚗
Delivery

Final quality inspection, walkthrough with the client, and care instruction briefing.
