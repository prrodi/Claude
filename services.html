Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does paint correction take?
+
Depending on the level of correction required and the size of the vehicle, paint correction typically takes 6–16 hours. Severe cases or very large vehicles may require a second day. We always assess the paint beforehand and give you a realistic timeline.
How long does ceramic coating last?
+
Our ceramic coatings range from 2-year to 5-year protection depending on the product selected. Longevity is also influenced by maintenance. We recommend a maintenance wash every 4–6 weeks and an annual inspection.
Is my exotic car safe in your hands?
+
Absolutely. We have extensive experience with Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, Rolls-Royce, Bentley, Porsche, and other exotic marques. Our technicians are trained in exotic-specific protocols, and every vehicle is fully insured while in our care.
Do I need paint correction before ceramic coating?
+
Yes — ceramic coating locks in the current condition of your paint. Applying it over scratches or swirls would seal those defects permanently. We always recommend at least a 1-stage polish before ceramic coating to ensure the best possible result.
Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?
+
Yes. Every service is backed by our satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our work, contact us within 48 hours and we will address it immediately at no cost to you.
